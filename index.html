About Me

I'm a recent graduate from Quarry Lane School and a freshman Regents' and Chancellor's Scholar at the University of California, Berkeley. I am also an ambadassor for Yuvsatta, an an international nonprofit working to support underprivileged youth in marginalized areas, where I worked to girls to expose them to Computer Science. I enjoy problem solving, public speaking, and volunteering to ultimately be the change we seek in the world.

Everybody Code Now!, Founder & Executive Director

I was able to discover my passion for Computer Science thanks to TJ and an incredible group of mentors that encouraged me along the way. It was important to me that such opportunities existed for everyone from a young age.

That's why I founded Everybody Code Now! and serve as the Executive Director. So far, we have exposed more than 1000 students to Computer Science and helped schools raise more than $35,000 to fund future STEM activities in schools.

If you're interested in learning more or working together, please visit our website at everybodycodenow.org.

Ashburn Teen Leadership Council, Director

I served as the Director of the Ashburn Teen Leadership Council for five years, working with area youth and the Ashburn Library to improve teen engagement and encourage teens to do community service. I was elected/re-elected each year and served through August 2017.
More information about the Council can be found at ashburntlc.weebly.com.

Carson scholarship honors teens who demonstrate humanitarian qualities and have outstanding academic achievement. Won the scholarship in 2015 and was again honored as a recognized scholar in 2016.
